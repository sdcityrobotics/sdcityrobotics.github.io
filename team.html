--- layout: team title: Team permalink: /team pageimage: /images/team2018.jpg --- {% comment %} NOTE: Edit team member information in /_data/team.yml {% endcomment %} San Diego City College Robotics team members are studying a variety of disciplines, including engineering, computer science, and physics. We compete in the RoboSub competition, which is held at the SPAWAR Systems Center Pacific TRANSDEC facility in San Diego. Our team designs, builds, tests, and deploys an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) for the competition. The vehicle is designed to complete a series of tasks using information provided by its acoustic and visual sensors.
